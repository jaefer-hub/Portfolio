About

Language Operations Built Around Quality

My work sits at the intersection of human linguistic expertise and modern AI language systems. I specialize in evaluating, refining, and quality-assuring content that passes through automated translation and generation pipelines — ensuring it meets the standard actual humans expect.

Operating across Arabic, English, and Tigrinya, I bring structured workflow discipline to every project: from reviewing AI-generated responses for coherence and cultural accuracy, to post-editing machine translations that need linguistic precision before reaching end users.

I work with localization agencies, AI data companies, and freelance platforms to deliver consistent, measurable language quality — not subjective impressions.
