I am a freelance linguist based in Giza, Egypt, specializing in Arabic and Tigrinya language services for international clients. My native language combination — Arabic and Tigrinya — is one that very few professional linguists worldwide can offer, which gives me a real edge on projects that require both.

My six years of experience span real project work across multiple domains: medical records and clinical trials, legal contracts, financial documentation, technical manuals, software localization, YouTube and interview transcription, machine translation post-editing, and AI training data evaluation. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Translation and Linguistics from the University of Asmara, and I have worked directly with Frontier AI as a certified AI Training Expert.

On Upwork I maintain a high job success score with consistently positive client feedback across multiple long-term contracts. On Translated.com my satisfaction rate is 98–100%. I use SDL Trados, memoQ, Smartcat, and Wordfast on every project and maintain client-specific glossaries throughout all ongoing work. I also volunteer as a translator with UNHCR Egypt, providing interpretation across four languages for refugee programs serving 500+ families.

What I bring is not just language knowledge — it is the judgment to know when a literal translation fails the reader, when a term needs a specialist, and when a deadline cannot move. Clients come back because the work is done right.
