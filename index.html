About
Who
I Am
Arabic — Native Tigrinya — Native English — Fluent Amharic — Fluent

I am a freelance linguist based in Giza, Egypt, working with international clients across translation, transcription, localization, and AI language evaluation. My native languages are Arabic and Tigrinya — a combination that very few professionals can offer — and I work fluently in English and Amharic across all project types.

My background covers six years of real project work: medical records, legal contracts, technical documentation, YouTube and interview transcription, machine translation post-editing, and AI training data evaluation. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Translation and Linguistics from the University of Asmara, and I have worked directly with Frontier AI as a certified AI Training Expert.

I work through Upwork and Translated.com, where my client satisfaction consistently reaches 98–100%. I use SDL Trados, memoQ, Smartcat, and Wordfast, and I maintain client-specific glossaries on every ongoing project. I also volunteer as a translator with UNHCR Egypt, supporting refugee families across four languages.

What I bring is not just language knowledge — it is the judgment to know when a literal translation fails the reader, when a term needs a subject matter expert, and when a deadline cannot move. Clients come back because the work is done right.
